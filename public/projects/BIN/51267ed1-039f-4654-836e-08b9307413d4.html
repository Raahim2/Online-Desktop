Classic Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • 400g (14oz) Spaghetti or your favorite pasta
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g / 14oz) Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black Pepper, to taste
  • Fresh Basil leaves, for garnish
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain, reserving about 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute (be careful not to burn it).
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, and red pepper flakes (if using). Bring the sauce to a simmer.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let the sauce simmer gently for at least 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the sauce. Toss well to coat the pasta evenly. If the sauce seems too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water, one tablespoon at a time, until it reaches your desired consistency.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
