About Flavor Network

The Flavor Network project aims to visualize the fascinating relationships between different food ingredients. Based on data analysis of thousands of recipes, we can identify ingredients that frequently appear together, suggesting strong flavor affinities.

This interactive tool provides a simplified glimpse into that network. By selecting an ingredient, you can explore its most common partners, helping you brainstorm new recipe combinations or understand the structure of existing dishes.

Currently, this demonstration uses a limited, predefined dataset for illustrative purposes. A full implementation would involve a much larger database and potentially more complex visualization techniques.
