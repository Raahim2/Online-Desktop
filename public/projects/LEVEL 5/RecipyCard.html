Classic Fluffy Pancakes

Prep time: 10 mins Cook time: 15 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Maple syrup, for serving
  • Fresh berries, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until just smooth. Do not overmix.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  3. Brown on both sides until bubbles form and begin to pop on the surface (about 2-3 minutes per side). Flip carefully with a thin spatula.
  4. Serve hot with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, butter, and fresh berries. Enjoy your delicious homemade pancakes!
